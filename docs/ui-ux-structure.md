# UI / UX Structure

## Experience Layers

- Marketing: narrative-driven landing pages with strong B2B and AI positioning
- Commerce: product discovery, comparison, checkout, and post-purchase trust
- Customer: order tracking, rewards, warranty vault, QR passport
- Admin: dense operational interface for CRM, catalog, and analytics

## Primary Navigation

- Home
- Products
- AI Mattress Finder
- Sleep Health Center
- B2B Solutions
- Tender Solutions
- Blog
- Contact
- Sign In

## Layout Principles

- Warm, premium sleep aesthetic using sand, forest, slate, and sunrise tones
- High-contrast CTAs for quote generation and consultation bookings
- Modular cards, KPI panels, calculators, and insight rails
- Responsive dashboard shells with sticky context actions

## Key Screens

- Home: positioning, proof points, AI workflows, vertical solutions
- Products: filters, comparison, finance calculator, review summaries
- Product Details: variants, warranty, QR passport, cross-sell, reviews
- Customer Dashboard: orders, coins, referrals, warranty vault, consultant chat
- Admin Dashboard: revenue, leads, stock health, loyalty, warranty claims
